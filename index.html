ABOUT US

Silverware is a dreamy indie rock group based out of Victoria BC. We are a group of four with lead vocalist Huck on guitar, lead/rhythm guitar Jake Styles, Bassist Cole Davis, and Drummer Ava Creed. Heavy effects use across the band has created a signature sound for the band which matches the often upbeat but deep lyrics based approach of their songs.
