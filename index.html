ABOUT US

hi! We're Silverware
A dreamy indie rock group from Victoria BC. We are a group of four with lead vocalist Huckleberry Flett on guitar, lead/rhythm guitarist Jake Styles, Bassist Cole Davis, and Drummer Ava Creed. Thanks for checking out our website! You can find examples of our music as well as upcoming shows, and if you feel so inclined theres a contact sheet if you'd like to reach out to us! Enjoy your visit :

OUR VIBE

We like to describe ourselves as a dreamy indie project, however we dont take ourselves all that seriously. at a Silverware show or on an album, you can expect heartfelt lyrics and catchy dreamy guitar riffs with lots of effects. Off the stage, and between songs expect silly antics and endless bits that'll be thought provoking.

FUTURE PLANS

It's our dream to do music as a career. We're obviously a long way from that but hey, baby steps. As of right now we're looking at planning our first tour, as well as releasing our first official EP! Keep checking back here on our website to keep up to date on all things Silverware!

THE CREW

The Degenerat-I mean Musicians that make up Silverware are pictured below. Huckleberry Flett writes many of the lyrics and builds the initial framework for a lot of the songs. He also handles the bands social media as well as promotional material for shows/musical releases.Cole Davis Handles bone rattling bass lines and carries the band in terms of production. Jake Styles slays the guitar during solos and rhythm parts, He also does the artwork for the band (he even coded this website!). Ava Creed lays it DOWN on the drums, she's responsible for keeping the band together during shows and is key to the bands dynamics. She is also a Photographer/Videographer, and is responsible for many of the band's photos!
